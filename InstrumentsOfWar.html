    Compose yourself, and your surroundings! In a world where

    that engages in competitive 1v1s to test various weapons manufactured by the
    conservatory, a cloud base laboratory. Utilize movement mechanics such as wallrunning, sliding, dashing, and more to dominate your opponent!
    Customize your arsenal and aesthetic to your liking.
